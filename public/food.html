Brownies

During a virtual happy hour for work I made homemade brownies. They were super easy to make. All you need is 3/4 cup flour, 2/3 cup cocoa powder, 3/4 tsp salt, 1 cup sugar(then mix before wet ingredients), 2 eggs, 1/2 cup vegetable oil, 1 tsp vanilla extract, 2 tbsp water, and whatever mix ins you want. I chopped up a chocolate bar for a mix in and topping. I'd say I'll never use boxed mix again but that's a lie even though these came out delicious.
